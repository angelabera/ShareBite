Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate surplus food?

You can register your restaurant/household and schedule a pickup. Our partnered NGOs and volunteers will collect and distribute the food.

How do NGOs join the platform?

NGOs can sign up through our registration form and get verified before they can start receiving donations. The registration form is available at the NGO Register page mentioned in the footer.

Can volunteers join individually?

Is there a cost to use ShareBite?

No, ShareBite is a Free platform dedicated to fighting food waste and hunger. Our services for donors, NGOs, and volunteers are completely free of charge.
