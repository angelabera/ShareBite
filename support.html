Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

For Donors

How do I list food for donation?

Click the "Add Listing" button on the main page, fill in details about the food type, quantity, pickup location, and preferred pickup time. You can also add photos to help recipients see what's available.

What types of food can I donate?

You can donate fresh, properly stored leftover food including prepared meals, baked goods, packaged foods, and fresh produce. All food must be safe for consumption and meet our food safety guidelines.

How long does food stay listed?

Food listings remain active until the "Fresh Until" time you specify, or until someone claims it. You can also manually remove listings at any time.

For Recipients

How do I claim food donations?

Browse available listings, click "Claim" on any item you'd like to collect, and contact the donor to coordinate pickup. Make sure to arrive during the specified pickup time window.

Do I need to register as an NGO?

NGOs and charitable organizations should register through our NGO portal for verified status. Individual volunteers can use the regular user registration.

Is there a limit to how much I can claim?

There's no limit, but we encourage responsible claiming. Only claim what you can reasonably collect and distribute to avoid waste.
