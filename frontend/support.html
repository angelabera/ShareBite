How can NGOs and volunteers join the platform?

NGOs register on the NGO registration page and go through a quick verification process (documents + review). Individuals can sign up as volunteers and browse/claim available listings directly (subject to donor approval and safety checks).

What kind of food is accepted and how is safety ensured?

We accept fresh, edible surplus food (cooked meals, bakery items, fruits, vegetables, unopened packaged food within expiry). Expired, spoiled or opened perishables are not allowed. Donors must provide preparation time & freshness window; collectors follow hygiene guidelines and can refuse suspicious donations.
